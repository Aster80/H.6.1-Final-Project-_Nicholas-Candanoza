# Reflection

## About My Process

### What was your biggest "aha moment"?

My biggest realization was that AI is much more useful when I give clear and structured instructions. The more detail I included, the better the results became.

### Describe a time AI gave you something useless.

There were times when the AI gave me incorrect calculations or code that didn’t run. I had to go back, test things myself, and ask more specific follow-up questions to fix the problems.

### How did your prompting change?

At first, my prompts were basic and unclear. Over time, I started including exact requirements, expected outputs, and examples, which helped improve the quality of responses.

## What I Learned

### One thing you can do now

I can now use AI to help build applications, debug code, and generate working solutions for programming problems.

### What surprised you?

I was surprised by how helpful AI is for speeding up development, but also how important it is to double-check its work.

### What is AI bad at?

AI can struggle with accuracy and sometimes gives incorrect or incomplete solutions, especially for more complex problems.
